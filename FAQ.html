What if I am having technical difficulties and cannot join the match?

Notify your opponent immediately that you are trying to connect. If issues come up, send either a support or protest ticket to ESL through their website.

How can I report my opponent for cheating or other types of misconduct?

Go to the tournament-specific page on ESL’s website and create a protest against your opponent. Provide screenshots and evidence as necessary.

What does it mean when I receive a “bye”?

A “bye” means that you do not play in this round of the tournament and do not have an opponent. This means you will automatically advance to the next round. Wait for a notification for your next match once you receive a bye.

How do I know when my next match starts?

You will receive a notification when your next match is ready. It will also appear in the Events app.
